New Practices

Starting a New Practice? We'll Get You Credentialed Fast.

Starting a medical practice is exciting — but navigating the credentialing process for the first time can be overwhelming and time-consuming. Applications are lengthy, requirements differ by payer, and a single error can delay your enrollment by months.

Xpert Billing takes the entire credentialing burden off your plate. From NPI setup to CAQH profile creation to submitting applications to every payer you want to work with, we handle every step — so your first claim can be submitted from day one.

1

Information Gathering

We collect your licenses, DEA, malpractice, education history, and specialty certifications securely.

2

CAQH Profile Creation & Setup

We create and complete your CAQH profile, the universal credentialing database used by most commercial payers.

3

Payer Application Submission

We submit your enrollment applications to all selected payers — Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial insurers.

4

Follow-Up & Tracking

We actively track every application, respond to payer requests, and keep you updated throughout the process.

5

Confirmation & Billing Setup

Once approved, we set up your billing system and you're ready to submit claims and collect reimbursements.

PT Billing Expertise

Specialized Billing Built for Physical Therapy Practices

Physical therapy practices face billing requirements unlike any other specialty. From the 8-minute rule for timed CPT codes to Medicare's therapy cap exceptions, even a small coding error can result in significant revenue loss or compliance risk.

Our PT billing specialists are trained specifically in outpatient rehabilitation billing — understanding the nuances that generic billers miss, and ensuring your claims are accurate, compliant, and optimized for maximum reimbursement.

8-Minute Rule Compliance

Accurate timed unit billing to maximize reimbursement without compliance risk.

Medicare Cap Management

KX modifier application and therapy threshold tracking for all Medicare patients.

Authorization Management

We track and manage authorizations across all payers to prevent mid-treatment denials.

Denial Prevention & Appeals

Proactive claim scrubbing and aggressive denial appeals specific to PT payer behavior.
